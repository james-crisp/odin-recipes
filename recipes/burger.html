Burger Recipe

Description

Burgers are juicy and delicious and satiating, and are worth making right. Here is a wonderful burger recipe that I made up!

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat
  • Acocado
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Pickles
  • Spicy Mayo Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground meat on grill.
  2. Heat buns in toaster oven.
  3. Place burger on mayo covered buns.
  4. Place lettuce, avocados and pickles to your liking.
  5. Add spicy mayo sauce under top bun.
