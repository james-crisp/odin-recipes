Pizza Recipe

Description

I love pizza with its excellent crunchy and doughy crust, its delicious marina sauce and cheese sprinkled on top with beautiful garnishes like cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Spread dough onto pan in a circular shape.
  2. Spread marina sauce onto dough.
  3. Sprinkle cheese to desire onto marina sauce.
  4. Bake pizza in oven.
  5. Garnish with cilantro to your taste.
