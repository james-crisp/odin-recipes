Dragon Roll Sushi Recipe

Description

Dragon rolls are one of my favorite types of sushi. They typically include avocado and crunchy shrimp tempura. Sometimes there is eel and a delicious eel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp Tempura
  • Acocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori (Seaweed)
  • Spicy Mayo
  • Unagi Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut cucumbers.
  2. Remove skin and cut avocados.
  3. Line bamboo mat with plastic wrap and spread sushi rice with vinegar water.
  4. Place two pieces of shrimp tempura, two cucumber strips on the end of the nori sheet.
  5. Start rolling nori sheet tightly and firmly and squeeze the roll.
  6. Remove bamboot mat and place avocado slices on top of sushi roll.
  7. Place rolls under plastic layer and cut, then sprinkle spicy mayo on top after removing plastic film.
