Dragon Roll Sushi Recipe

Description

Dragon rolls are one of my favorite types of sushi. They typically include avocado and crunchy shrimp tempura. Sometimes there is eel and a delicious eel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp Tempura
  • Acocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori (Seaweed)
  • Spicy Mayo
  • Unagi Sauce
